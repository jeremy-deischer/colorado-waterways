About this map

The Flood Control District partners with local jurisdictions to design and construct flood control, open space, trails projects in the metro area.

Density per basin was calculated from the completed projects shapefile provided by UDFCD.

National Hydrology Dataset basins were used to summarize the number of projects within each basin.
